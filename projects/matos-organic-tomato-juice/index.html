Mato's Organic Tomato Juice
Brand Identity & Art Direction — 2026

Mato's is an organic tomato juice brand built around one idea: Radical Ripeness. Tomatoes harvested at peak season, sourced from farms across the European Union. The identity spans logo, packaging, typography, color, social media, and print — all designed to feel bold, unapologetic, and rooted in real agriculture.
